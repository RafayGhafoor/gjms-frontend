Global Journal of Management, Social Sciences and Humanities (GJMSSH) is a blind peer review quarterly journal. Its Editorial Board consists of experienced and renowned academicians, researchers and professionals, who have developed vision and mission as well as policy of journal. The aim of GJMSSH is to provide a platform to research community to share knowledge and exchange their innovative research work. The researchers from all over the world are invited to submit their research papers for publishing in this journal.
